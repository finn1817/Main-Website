📖 How to Play

🎯 Objective

Guess the hidden word letter by letter before the hangman drawing is completed!

🎮 Game Modes

  • Two Player: One player enters a word, the other guesses
  • VS Computer: Play against AI with different difficulty levels
  • Alternating: Take turns setting words for each other

⚡ Gameplay

  • Click letters to make guesses
  • Correct guesses reveal letters in the word
  • Wrong guesses add parts to the hangman drawing
  • You lose after 6 wrong guesses
  • Use up to 3 hints per game

🏆 Difficulty Levels

  • Easy: 4-6 letter words
  • Medium: 7-9 letter words
  • Hard: 10+ letter words
