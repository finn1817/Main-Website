📖 How to Play

🎯 Objective

Find all hidden words in the letter grid. Words can be placed horizontally, vertically, or diagonally in any direction!

🎮 How to Select Words

  • Mouse: Click and drag from the first letter to the last letter of a word
  • Touch: Tap and drag across the letters on mobile devices
  • Words can go in 8 directions: horizontal, vertical, and diagonal
  • Words can be forwards or backwards

⚡ Game Features

  • Timer: Track how long it takes to complete the puzzle
  • Hints: Get 3 hints per game to reveal word locations
  • Highlight: Briefly highlight all hidden words
  • Categories: Choose from different themed word lists

🏆 Difficulty Levels

  • Easy: 10x10 grid with 8 words
  • Medium: 15x15 grid with 12 words
  • Hard: 20x20 grid with 16 words
  • Custom: Create your own puzzle with custom words

📱 Tips

  • Look for common letter patterns
  • Check all 8 directions from each letter
  • Use hints sparingly for better scores
  • Words never overlap letters
