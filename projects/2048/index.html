📖 How to Play

🎯 Objective

Join the numbers and get to the 2048 tile to win!

🎮 How to Play

  • Desktop: Use arrow keys (↑↓←→) to move tiles
  • Mobile: Swipe in any direction or use the on-screen arrows
  • When two tiles with the same number touch, they merge into one!
  • After every move, a new tile appears randomly

⚡ Game Modes

  • Classic: Traditional 4x4 grid, reach 2048
  • Big Grid: 5x5 grid for longer gameplay
  • Huge Grid: 6x6 grid for maximum challenge
  • Speed Mode: Race against time with a 3-minute timer

🏆 Scoring

  • Points are earned when tiles merge
  • Higher value merges give more points
  • Try to create chains of merges for bonus points
  • Undo moves cost 10% of your current score

💡 Tips

  • Keep your highest tile in a corner
  • Build up tiles in one direction
  • Don't spread high-value tiles around
  • Plan several moves ahead
  • Use undo sparingly - it costs points!
